Introduction

I am a PhD student in Political Science at the University at Buffalo (SUNY), where I study the intersection of international conflict, civil-military relations, and foreign policy decision-making. My research asks how states manage the use of force — both internally and on the world stage. I completed my M.A. in Political Science at Sungkyunkwan University in Seoul, South Korea, and am always happy to discuss research, ideas, or potential collaborations.
